A New Kind of Instant Messaging

With the rise of government monitoring programs, Tox provides an easy to use application that allows you to connect with friends and family without anyone else listening in. While other big-name services require you to pay for features, Tox is totally free and comes without advertising.

About Tox

Tox is decentralized and uses secure end-to-end encryption. Allowing you to take your data back into your own hands, rather than trusting data mining corporations and goverment agencies. Securing from all, and yet with the ability to share private information both across the globe, and locally.
